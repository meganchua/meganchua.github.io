Two words.  

Passion and Intention.

Every action and decision I have made throughout my life has been inspired by these two words. No matter what job I am working, I embody these values. I believe they are what set leaders apart from followers and opportunities apart from obstacles. I work hard because I am passionate about my work. I deliver quality end-products because I have meaningful intentions behind every action.

My name is Megan Chua and I am a programmer based out of San Diego, California. I am a recent graduate of San Diego State University with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Interdisciplinary Studies. My fascination with programming and technology started in high school and blossomed in college as I took on new projects and found that my projects could make a positive impact on other people’s lives. I found a reason to put positive intentions behind my projects.

I have a creative soul in both my professional and personal life. I thrive in environments that encourage collaboration and honest feedback. I am constantly craving to learn new skills and hobbies in my spare time. A few of my long-time hobbies include singing, dancing, drawing, running, and reading.

Passion and intention are what push me to keep searching for new opportunities and to face challenges head-on. I hope to find an opportunity that engages both my creative and driven spirit.
